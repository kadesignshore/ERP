Youth Registration Form (-18 yrs)

ABC Football Club collects personal information, including sensitive information about children and parents each year for club purposes to enable us to carry out our 'Duty of Care' and to contact you if required. This on-line form must be submitted to the club before your children can participate in any of ABC sport activities.

The contact information is used to communicate with parents/guardians about program information using media such as emails, phone texting (SMS) and the club website abcfootballorg.au.

Health information about children is sensitive information within the terms of the National Privacy Policy under the Privacy Act. The only reason we ask for this is so that we can better care for your child. If we do not obtain this information we may not be able to care for your child correctly in the case of an emergency.

We wil not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

If circumstances change, it is the responsibility of the signatory to advise the club of full details.

We are a club that adheres to the WA Safe Child policy and screen all leaders before they are appointed. Leaders in child-related activitieis have a current WA Working with Children/Card Check.
